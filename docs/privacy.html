Anees TV — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 7 July 2026

Anees TV (“the app”, “we”) is a media player. It plays content that you provide by adding your own playlist (an M3U URL or Xtream Codes login). The app ships with no channels, movies, or media of its own, and we do not host, provide, sell, or resell any content or subscriptions.

What we collect and store

On your device (not sent to us):

  • Your playlist details (name, and either the M3U URL or your Xtream server / username / password) — stored locally so the app can load your content.
  • Your favorites, watch progress (“continue watching”), profiles, and settings.
  • Any downloads you choose to save, stored in the folder you pick.

We do not run our own accounts, and we do not collect your name, email, or location. We do not sell your data. We show no ads.

Information processed by third parties

  • Movie/TV metadata (TMDB): to show posters, ratings, and descriptions, the app sends a title/year lookup to The Movie Database (TMDB) — via our metadata proxy. No personal information is included in these requests. This product uses the TMDB API but is not endorsed or certified by TMDB.
  • Your IPTV provider: when you play, download, or load your playlist, the app connects directly to the server you configured, using the credentials you entered. We are not a party to that service and cannot see your viewing.
  • Purchases (RevenueCat + the app store): if you buy Anees TV Pro, the purchase is handled by Google Play / the App Store and RevenueCat. We receive an anonymous entitlement status (“Pro: yes/no”) and no payment details.
  • Crash reports (if enabled in a future version): anonymous crash/diagnostic data (error, device model, OS version) to help us fix bugs. No content or credentials are included.

Cloud sync (only if you enable it, future feature)

If a future version offers cross-device sync and you opt in, your profiles, favorites, watch progress, and playlist credentials would be stored in your private account, encrypted, and used only to sync your own devices — never indexed, aggregated, shared, or sold.

Children

Anees TV is a general-audience utility for playing content you supply. It does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your control

  • Clear your data any time: remove a playlist, clear viewing history, delete downloads, or uninstall the app (which removes all locally stored data).
  • Turn TMDB metadata off in Settings.

Changes

We may update this policy; the “last updated” date will change accordingly.
